UNC MTC Simplifier

Simplify Your Documents

Helping you reduce the complexity of your documents and better conform to the Plain Language Standards of the Federal Government.

Terms of Service Contact Us

A simple user-friendly UI makes working with this add-on incredibly easy. Our app refreshes on its own - so there is no hassle of clicking a button! A drop-down provides for easy access to all sections.

The <3000 ratio is the ratio of words in your document that are within the top 3000 words in the English Language. We also provide Flesh-Kincaid Grade Level and Flesh-Reading Ease scores.

We break down the word frequencies for the <3000 ratios in great detail. We also provide the type-token ratio which is the ratio of unique words to total words in the document.

We've provided instructions and contact info in case you run into any trouble. We hope you enjoy the app and feel free to reach out with any concerns or questions!
